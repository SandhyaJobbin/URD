Assessment Rules

60 seconds per question. Timer resets on each new review.

Four decisions: Publish, Moderation Remove, Member Fraud Remove, Property Fraud Remove.

For Remove decisions, select from 20 reasons in a dropdown. Your moderator comment is automatically pre-filled.

Questions are drawn randomly each session — reducing repetition risk.

Reviewer details are partially masked for privacy compliance.

A downloadable Certificate of Completion is issued for scores of ≥ 80%.
